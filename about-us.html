Our Story

Founded by husband-and-wife team Jason and Maria, Lucky Shrub was born from their shared dream of creating beautiful, functional landscapes that bring nature closer to home. Jason, the garden architect, brings years of experience and creativity to the table, overseeing the design process and managing the landscaping team. His eye for detail and passion for sustainable design set Lucky Shrub apart. Maria, on the other hand, manages the plant nursery and leads all marketing efforts, ensuring every project gets the personal touch it deserves.
